title: 原来几组ob_start/ob_get_contents是可以嵌套的
category: php
---

一直在使用ob_start和ob_get_contents时非常小心，避免嵌套使用，今天试了一下，原来几组ob_start/ob_get_contents是可以嵌套的。下面是例子：


<?php
&nbsp;ob_start();
&nbsp;?>
&nbsp;<html>
&nbsp;<body>
&nbsp;<p>It's like comparing apples to oranges.</p>
&nbsp;<?
&nbsp;ob_start();
&nbsp;print("{{{{{{{{}}}}}}}}}}");
&nbsp;$str2 = ob_get_contents();
&nbsp;ob_end_clean();


?>


</body>
&nbsp;</html>
&nbsp;<?php
&nbsp;$str1 = ob_get_contents();
&nbsp;ob_end_clean();


var_dump($str1);


var_dump($str2);


?>


运行结果如下：


[~/]^o^:php test.php
string(77) "<html>
<body>
<p>It's like comparing apples to oranges.</p>


</body>
</html>
"
string(18) "{{{{{{{{}}}}}}}}}}"
